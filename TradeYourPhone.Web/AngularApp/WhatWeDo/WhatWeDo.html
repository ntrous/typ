We've made our process as simple as possible!

  • Simply fill out a quote by adding your phone(s) and filling out your personal details.

  • Then select which postage option you would like to use:

    • Free Tracked Satchel - We post a free Tracked AusPost satchel to the address you provide us. Simply package up your phone(s) place them in the satchel and send them to us!

      •
    • Post Yourself - This is the fastest option for getting your phone(s) to us. Simply package up your phone and send to our free reply-paid address!

  • Once we recieve your phone(s) we will assess them to make sure they match the condition you chose.

  • Then within 24 hours of receiving your phone, you get paid!
