Free Shipping

Trade Your Phone currently has two ways of sending your phones to us, Reply Paid Address or Satchel. Both are completely free, so just choose whatever option is easiest for you!

Reply-Paid Satchel

With this option we send you a Free Reply-Paid satchel that you put your phone inside and drop off at the post shop. That's it! No charge to you and it gets delivered straight to us!

Reply-Paid Address

You can also use your own packaging and send your phone to our free reply-paid address. This is by far the fastest option and lets you get paid for your phone faster! Simply package your phone up, write our reply-paid address on the front and drop off at the post shop. It gets sent straight to us at no cost to you!
