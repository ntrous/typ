What happens to the data on my phone?

When we receive your phone, we reset it back to factory settings whether or not it has already been reset. We guanrantee no personal data will be left on the device.

Why does Trade Your Phone want my phone?

So we can help people who can’t afford brand new phones get a decent used mobile! And, of course, so we can turn enough of a profit to keep going.

Do you accept faulty/broken phones?

Yes we do! When filling out a quote, select Faulty as the condition type to get a price for your faulty/broken device.

Will you accept my phone if it has been stolen?

No. We do not accept stolen phones. If we recieve your phone and it turns out it is stolen, we are obligated to hand the phone and your details over to the police.
