Postage

How long does it take for the Satchel to arrive to me?

Your satchel should only take 2-3 days to arrive but if it has been longer than 5 business days since you were notified the satchel was sent, please go to our contact page and let us know. We will sort it out as soon as possible.

How long will it take for my phone to arrive at Trade Your Phone once I send it?

Once you post your phone(s) they will take between 2-5 business days to arrive. If it has been longer than 5 business days, please contact us and we will do what we can to sort it out.

I sent my phone but I selected the wrong Model or Condition. What should I do?

Not to worry! Once we recieve your phone(s) we will assess them to ensure they match the model and condition you selected. If your device turns out to match a different model or condition than what was selected, we will contact you with a revised offer at which point you can choose to accept or get your device back.

Does the free Satchel come with tracking?

It sure does! Once you send the satchel back to us you will be able to watch its progress with the tracking number and see exactly when it arrives at Trade Your Phone!
